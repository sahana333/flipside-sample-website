What we do?

PROVEN AND TRUSTED, BEST IN CLASS LABELING

Flipside is a pioneer and leader in labeling for computer vision and perception, particularly for autonomous vehicles (AV), advanced driver assistance systems (ADAS) and robotics. A true partner to both our customers and labelers, we operate an outsourced business process outsourcing (BPO) service to ensure high-accuracy, secure and ethical data labeling.

Flipside Digital Content was founded in 2010 and operates as Philippines-based BPO, now specialized in providing outsourced AI data labeling services as Flipside AI after 8 years as an ebook production house

MISSION

Our mission at Flipside AI is to empower organizations with accurate, scalable, and efficient AI data labeling solutions. We strive to bridge the gap between humans and machines by combining our expertise in AI with human intelligence to provide the highest quality data labeling services. Our goal is to help our clients unlock the full potential of their AI and machine learning initiatives while enabling them to make more informed decisions with the power of data.

VISION

Our vision at Flipside AI is to become the leading provider of ethical AI data labeling solutions that enhance the capabilities of machine learning and AI models. We aim to help create a future where AI and machine learning are accessible to all organizations, enabling them to solve complex problems and drive innovation. We aspire to continuously innovate and leverage the latest advancements in AI technology to deliver the most efficient and reliable data labeling services, helping our clients unlock the full potential of their AI initiatives.
